Count Modifier

You want MORE mobs from the spawner? No problem! Just make sure there's space for all these mobs!

Right Click a spawner with a to increase it's maximum spawn count by 1. Range: 1-20
Right Click a spawner with a to increase it's maximum amount of nearby mobs by 2. Range: 1-200
Right Click a spawner with a to increase it's maximum amount of nearby mobs to the maximum of 32767, far past the limit.

Spawn Count will usually be A LOT less than the maximum. This is due to the fact that spawn attempts may fail and the spawn count is also capped by the maximum amount of nearby mobs of the same type.

The reason the maximum amount of nearby mobs is capped at 32767 is because the amount in the spawner's nbt is a short, which only uses 2 bytes (16 bits) AND for some reason, the number is signed, which removes 1 bit that you can work with.
