Reflective Defenses

Hate weak mobs bothering your shield? Well take some revenge with this enchantment!

On a successful block, there's a 1 / (7 - level) chance to damage the attacker for 15% of the blocked damage or 5 damage (2.5 hearts), whichever is higher.
This comes at a cost of 10 durability of the shield.

Available for shields. 5 levels.
