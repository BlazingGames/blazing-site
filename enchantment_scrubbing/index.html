Enchantment Scrubbing

Accidentally got the wrong enchantment? Uhh, yeah so you can fix that.

Add or in a grindstone to remove a specific enchantment from a tool.
Use a to remove vanilla enchantments.
Use a to remove custom enchantments.
The amount of the sponges you put in MUST be equal to the 1-based index of the enchantment in the space of all enchantments on the item you can remove with that type of sponge!
Right now, you have to reinsert the sponge each time you want to remove an enchantment for some reason... Also, this process gives you a book with the enchantment that was removed from the tool.
