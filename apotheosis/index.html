Apotheosis

Modifying Spawners, You modify spawners by holding one of the listed items in your hand and rightclicking the spawner with them, we also added support for placing mob spawn eggs inside spawners which is useful for the capturing enchantment.

Modifier Items

  • Sugar, Decrease min spawner delay.
  • Clock, Decrease max spawner delay.
  • Fermented Spider Eye, Increase spawn count.
  • Ghast Tear, Increase max nearby entities.
  • Prismarine Crystal, Increase required player range.
  • Blaze Rod, Decrease spawn range.
  • Nether Star, Ignore nearby player requirement. (sets range to 32K)
  • Quartz, If held in your offhand inverts the effects of other items.
