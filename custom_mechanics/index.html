Custom Mechanics

Craftable XP Bottles, Partly because of the custom enchants and partly because it's just useful we added craftable xp bottles. To "craft" the xp bottles you click an empty bottle while you have levels, it drains 1 of your levels to get you an xp bottle.

Silk Touch Spawners, Something that should be in vanilla anyways we added it. With the silk touch enchantment on a pickaxe of iron quality or higher you can break spawners and get them in your inventory, all the metadata stays so if you modified it previously you don't need to again.

Leadable Villagers, Moving villagers can be a pain, so we made them leadable just shift rclick a villager with a lead in your hand and you have your very own slave pet villager.
