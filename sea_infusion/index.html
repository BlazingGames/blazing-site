Sea Infusion

I mean, you can use this if you are raiding a lot of Ocean Monuments or killing a lot of drowneds... OR it just so happens to be raining!

Deals level² / 2 more damage (level² / 4 hearts) while the mob is in water or a bubble column or in rain.

Available for swords and tridents. 3 levels.
Incompatible with Hell Infusion, Bane of Illagers, Sharpness, Bane of Arthropods and Smite.
