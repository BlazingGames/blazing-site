Range Modifier

You want these spawners to use more space? Or do you want the spawners to be activated from further away?
You'll get that with this modifier.

Right Click a spawner with a to increase it's spawn range. Range: 1-20
Right Click a spawner with a to increase it's required player range. Range: 1-50
Right Click a spawner with a to increase it's required player range to the maximum of 32767, far past the limit.

The reason the spawn range is capped at 32767 is because the range in the spawner's nbt is a short, which only uses 2 bytes (16 bits) AND for some reason, the number is signed, which removes 1 bit that you can work with.
