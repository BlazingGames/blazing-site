Changes to the Vanilla Enchantments
With the rework of the enchanting system, many enchantments received recipes that need to be documented. Also some enchantments may have been tweaked. This page is gonna document them all.
Combat Enchantments
Bane of Arthropods
5 levels. Available on swords, axes and maces. Incompatible with Sharpness, Density, Breach, Bane of Arthropods, Hell Infusion, Sea Infusion or Bane of Illagers. Deals 2.5 * level more damage (1.25 * level hearts) to arthropods. Also inflicts Slowness IV on a hit arthropod for a duration ranging from 1 second to 1 + 0.5 * level seconds.
