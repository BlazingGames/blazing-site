Changes to the Vanilla Enchantments
With the rework of the enchanting system, many enchantments received recipes that need to be documented. Also some enchantments may have been tweaked. This page is gonna document them all.
Combat Enchantments
Smite
5 levels. Available on swords, axes and maces. Incompatible with Sharpness or Bane of Arthropods. Deals 2.5 * level more damage (1.25 * level hearts) to undead mobs.
1,1,1,10,rotten_flesh
2,2,2,20,rotten_flesh
3,3,3,30,rotten_flesh
4,4,4,40,rotten_flesh
4,5,5,50,rotten_flesh

Ranged Enchantments
Infinity
Available on bows. Incompatible with Mending. Regular arrows shot by this arrow don't get removed.
4,1,8,64,arrow

3 levels. Available on tridents. Incompatible with Riptide. After the trident hits something, it returns with a speed of level * 10 blocks per second.
