Enchantments

Custom Enchanting Table, We added a custom enchanting table so you could en chant your items with out custom enchants, to get it you just need to make an enchantmant altar instead of a standart enchanting layout. To make the altar build a 3x3 out of smooth quartz and place the enchanting table on top of the middle block, then add quartz stairs to all 4 sides, now for the corners place 3 obisidian pillars upward and then you can start enchanting.

Custom Enchanting, To add a custom enchant to a tool or gear piece you need to throw 1 lapis, 1 xp bottle (craftable), and the item you want to enchant on the enchanting table. Do note however that enchants can fail, the only thing that happens is a sound effect and the lapis and xp diddspears, you don't lose the item. There are 2 ways an enchant can fail, 1 is losing the 50/50 when you throw the items on, the second is if there are no enchantments left for your tool.

Enchants, There are currently 4 different enchants, this is a list of their names and what they do:

  • Tree Feller, cut down trees in 1 swing.
  • Collectable, nothing is out of reach ever again.
  • Capturing, so that's how mobs hatch.
  • Bane Of Illagers, never be bothered by illagers again.
