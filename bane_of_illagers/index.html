Bane of Illagers

Hate Illagers? Put this enchantment on your sword!

Deals 2.5 * level more damage (1.25 * level hearts) to Illagers.

Available for swords. 5 levels. Incompatible with Sea Infusion, Hell Infusion, Sharpness, Bane of Arthropods and Smite.
