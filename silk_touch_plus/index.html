Silk Touch+

Ever needed to move a spawner? Or maybe you just don't like NOT being able to harvest chiseled bookshelves without silk touch?
Don't worry! This plugin fixes both of your issues!

To get a spawner, mine it with an iron pickaxe or higher. The pickaxe MUST have silk touch.
You also no longer need silk touch to make chiseled bookshelves drop themselves.

All of the modifications you made to the spawner are saved.
