Altar of Enchanting - Tier 1 - Tier 2 - Tier 3 - Tier 4 - Tier 5

The altar is the main way that you can get enchantments and custom enchantments with this plugin. There are 5 tiers of the altar. This altar can perform the enchanting at a tier up to the altar's tier.

Right Click the to open up a new inventory. In this inventory, you can select which enchantments you want to apply to your item.
Put a tool into the tool slot, some into the lapis slot and some extra materials into the extra material slot, then select the enchantment you want on the right to enchant the tool with that enchantment.
The materials and levels required are different for each level of each enchantment.
Also each level of each enchantment requires the altar to be of a specific tier or higher.
