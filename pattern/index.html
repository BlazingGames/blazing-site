BlazingSite
Pattern

Tired of only being able to destroy one block at a time? Get this enchantment!

Allows you to mine a region of blocks with just one block broken! The area mined is dependent on the level of enchantment.

Available for pickaxes, axes, shovels and hoes. 3 levels.
Incompatible with Tree Feller.
